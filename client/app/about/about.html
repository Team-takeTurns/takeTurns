Project Overview

A web application that runs on browser. It is suitable for any device: desktop, tablet, or/and mobile devices.

Functionality:

1. Allows scheduling turns among multiple user for one shared object. For example, taking care of someone, sharing a car, etc…
2. Allows to send emails with various data about the events

Features:

1. A web application for a desktop, tablet, or mobile device
2. Simple navigation
3. User-friendly view (monthly, weekly, daily)
4. No sign-up required

It is a final semester school project of a three year program. However, the customer is real and the application is used by the customer for the purpose it is created.

Background

A family is looking for a solution that would allow easy scheduling of turns in taking care of their father who suffers from Alzheimer’s. Currently the family is scheduling activities via email which tends to be complicated. Emails address various topics and sometimes on opposite there multiple threads of the same email subject. Emails get lost and the schedule is in the form of text which does not provide a friendly view of the events. The family members use a variety of devices, ranging from cellphones, tablets and to desktops, of different brands and have different e-mail providers.
